HDS refers to a type of silica with superior dispersibility and compatibility in various applications, making it ideal for use in industries such as rubber and plastics. On the other hand, EDS silica is known for its ease of dispersion and handling, making it a preferred choice for applications where quick and efficient dispersibility is essential. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
