<p><strong>The Hair Transplant Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FUT</li><li>EUE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hair Transplant Technology Market includes two major types: FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation) and FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction). FUT involves removing a strip of tissue from the donor area and then dissecting it into individual follicular units for transplantation. FUE, on the other hand, involves extracting individual follicular units directly from the donor area using small punches. Both techniques aim to restore hair growth in individuals experiencing hair loss, offering different approaches to harvesting and transplanting hair follicles.</p></p>
