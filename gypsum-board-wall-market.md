<p><p>Gypsum board walls come in two main types: Gypsum Plaster Boards and Gypsum Fiber Boards. Gypsum plaster boards are made of a core of gypsum plaster sandwiched between layers of durable paper, providing a smooth surface for painting or wallpapering. Gypsum fiber boards, on the other hand, are reinforced with cellulose fibers, making them more resistant to moisture and mold. Both types of gypsum board walls are popular choices for construction due to their fire resistance, durability, and ease of installation.</p></p>
