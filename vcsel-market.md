<p><p>VCSEL technology is used in both single mode and multimode markets. Single mode VCSELs are designed for long-distance communication with a single light mode, allowing for greater transmission distances. On the other hand, multimode VCSELs are used for shorter distances and have multiple light modes, enabling them to carry more data at once. Single mode VCSELs are ideal for applications like data centers, while multimode VCSELs are commonly used in local area networks and short-distance communications.</p></p>
