<p><p>The Vehicle ISOFIX Anchorage Market includes different types based on the position of the child restraint system (CRS) in the vehicle. Forward facing CRS are designed for older children who face towards the front of the car and are secured using ISOFIX anchorages. Backward facing CRS are meant for infants and toddlers who face the rear of the vehicle and also utilize ISOFIX anchorage points for installation. These market types cater to varying age groups and safety needs of children in vehicles.</p></p>
