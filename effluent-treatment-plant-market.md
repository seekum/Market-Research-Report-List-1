<p><strong>The Effluent Treatment Plant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Preliminary Treatment</li><li>Secondary or Biological Treatment</li><li>Tertiary or Advanced or Disinfection Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP) market comprises three main types: Preliminary Treatment, Secondary or Biological Treatment, and Tertiary or Advanced or Disinfection Treatment. Preliminary Treatment involves the removal of large solids and debris from wastewater. Secondary or Biological Treatment employs biological processes to remove biological contaminants. Lastly, Tertiary or Advanced or Disinfection Treatment utilizes advanced methods like filtration, UV disinfection, or chemical processes to eliminate remaining impurities from wastewater. These different treatment processes are essential for ensuring the safe disposal or reuse of wastewater, addressing environmental concerns, and meeting regulatory requirements.</p></p>
