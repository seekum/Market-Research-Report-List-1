<p><ul><li>HPHT</li><li>CVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Synthetic Single Crystal Diamond market is divided into two main types: High Pressure High Temperature (HPHT) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). HPHT diamonds are created under extreme pressure and high temperatures, mimicking the natural diamond formation process. CVD diamonds, on the other hand, are grown using a chemical vapor deposition process. Both types of synthetic diamonds are used in various industries such as electronics, cutting tools, and jewelry due to their high quality, purity, and durability.</p></p>
