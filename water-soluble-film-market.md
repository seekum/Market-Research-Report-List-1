<p><ul><li>Cold Water-soluble Film</li><li>Hot Water-soluble Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-soluble films are classified into two types: cold water-soluble film and hot water-soluble film. Cold water-soluble films dissolve in cold water, making them suitable for applications where low temperatures are required, such as in the food industry. On the other hand, hot water-soluble films dissolve in hot water, offering faster disintegration for applications like detergent pods and agricultural products. These two types cater to different industries and provide unique advantages based on their solubility properties.</p></p>
