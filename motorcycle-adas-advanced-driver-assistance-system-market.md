<p><p>Motorcycle ADAS, or Advanced Driver Assistance Systems, includes various safety features to enhance the riding experience. Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during braking, Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) adjusts speed to maintain a safe following distance, Gear Shift Assist enables smoother shifting, Tire-Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) alerts riders of low tire pressure, and Traction Control System (TCS) prevents wheels from slipping during acceleration. These technologies improve rider safety and overall control of the motorcycle.</p></p>
