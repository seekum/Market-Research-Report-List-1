<p><p>Automotive heat shields come in various types to protect vehicles from extreme heat. Rigid heat shields are typically made of metal or composite materials and provide strong thermal protection. Flexible heat shields are made of materials like aluminum or stainless steel and can be easily manipulated to fit various vehicle parts. Textile heat shields are made of fabrics like fiberglass or ceramic fibers and offer lightweight and flexible thermal protection.
