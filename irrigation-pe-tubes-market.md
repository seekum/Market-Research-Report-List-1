<p><p>Irrigation PE tubes market types are classified based on the grade of the material used. PE80 tubes are made from medium density polyethylene with good flexibility and durability. PE100 tubes are made from high density polyethylene, offering higher strength and resistance to pressure. Farmers can choose the appropriate PE tube type based on their irrigation system requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
