<p><p>Metal Injection Molding (MIM) powder is a fine metal powder that is mixed with a polymer binder to create a feedstock for the MIM process. The MIM process involves molding the feedstock into a desired shape, debinding the polymer binder, and then sintering the metal powder to produce a final metal part with high precision and complex shapes.</p><p>The Metal Injection Molding Powder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by the increasing demand for complex and high-precision metal parts in industries such as automotive, aerospace, medical, and electronics.
