<p><p>Nonwovens for hygiene market types include Spunmelt Nonwovens, Spunbonded Nonwovens, and Spunlace Nonwovens. Spunmelt Nonwovens are made by combining spunbond and meltblown technologies, offering high strength and absorbency. Spunbonded Nonwovens are produced through a continuous process, providing excellent durability and barrier properties. Spunlace Nonwovens are created by entangling fibers with water jets, resulting in a soft and smooth texture. Each type offers unique characteristics suitable for various hygiene products such as diapers, feminine care products, and wipes.</p></p>
