<p><ul><li>Active Roll Control (ARC) System</li><li>Adaptive Samping System (ADS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Suspension-by-Wire market includes Active Roll Control (ARC) System and Adaptive Damping System (ADS). ARC System helps reduce body roll during cornering by applying hydraulic pressure to individual wheels. On the other hand, ADS adjusts the damping force of each shock absorber based on road conditions for a smoother driving experience. Both systems enhance vehicle stability, comfort, and handling by utilizing advanced electronic control technology to improve overall suspension performance.</p></p>
