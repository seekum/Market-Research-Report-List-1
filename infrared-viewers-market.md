<p><p>Infrared viewers can be categorized into two main types: handheld and post-mounted. Handheld infrared viewers are compact devices that are portable and easy to use for users who need to view objects or environments in low light conditions. On the other hand, post-mounted infrared viewers are fixed to a specific location, such as a surveillance system or a security checkpoint, providing continuous monitoring of a specific area. Both types of infrared viewers cater to different needs and applications in various industries such as security, defense, and surveillance.</p></p>
