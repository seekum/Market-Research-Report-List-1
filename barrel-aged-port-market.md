<p><p>Barrel-Aged Port comes in various types, with three popular varieties being Tawny Port, Colheita Port, and Garrafeira Port. Tawny Port is aged in small oak barrels, resulting in a smooth and nutty flavor profile. Colheita Port is a single vintage Tawny Port aged for at least seven years in barrel, showcasing the characteristics of that specific year. Garrafeira Port is a rare and high-quality style, aged for a minimum of eight years in barrel before being transferred to glass demijohns for further maturation.</p></p>
