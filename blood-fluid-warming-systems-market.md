<p><p>The Blood Fluid Warming Systems Market consists of three main types: Surface Warming Systems, Intravenous Warming Systems, and Patient Warming Accessories. Surface Warming Systems are designed to warm blood fluids externally, using devices such as blankets or pads. Intravenous Warming Systems, on the other hand, warm fluids directly by passing them through a warming device before entering the patient's body. Lastly, Patient Warming Accessories include various products like warming gowns or socks that are used to keep patients warm during medical procedures.</p></p>
