<p><p>Polypropylene Homopolymer (PPH) Resin Market Types include Isotactic Polypropylene, Atactic Polypropylene, and Syndiotactic Polypropylene. Isotactic Polypropylene has a high degree of crystallinity and is the most common type used in manufacturing. Atactic Polypropylene does not have a regular structure and is mainly used in adhesives and sealants. Syndiotactic Polypropylene has a unique structure with alternating stereochemistries and is known for its high melting point and stiffness. Each type has different characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
