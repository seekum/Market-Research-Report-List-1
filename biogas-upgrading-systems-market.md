<p><p>Biogas upgrading systems are utilized to enhance the quality of biogas produced from organic waste materials. Different types of biogas upgrading systems include membrane separation, pressure swing adsorption (PSA), water scrubber, chemical scrubber, and others. Membrane separation systems utilize permeable membranes to separate biogas components. PSA systems utilize adsorbents to separate gases. Water scrubbers use water to separate impurities from biogas. Chemical scrubbers use various chemicals for separation. Other types of systems may include cryogenic separation or physical absorption methods.</p></p>
