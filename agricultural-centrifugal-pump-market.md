<p><ul><li>Single-stage Pump</li><li>Multi-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural Centrifugal Pump Market Types include Single-stage Pump and Multi-stage Pump. Single-stage pumps have a simple design with only one impeller, suitable for low pressure and flow rate applications. On the other hand, Multi-stage pumps have multiple impellers stacked together in series, allowing for higher pressure and flow rates. They are ideal for applications that require pumping water over long distances or at high elevations. Both types cater to different agricultural needs based on factors such as pressure, flow rate, and distance of water transportation.</p></p>
