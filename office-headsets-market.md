<p><p>Office headsets come in three main market types based on their design: On the Ear, Over the Ear, and In the Ear. On the Ear headsets rest gently on the outer ear, providing comfort for long periods of wear. Over the Ear headsets feature ear cups that completely cover the ears, offering a secure fit and noise-cancellation. In the Ear headsets are compact and fit inside the ear canal, perfect for discreet communication. Each market type caters to different preferences for comfort, style, and functionality in the workplace.</p></p>
