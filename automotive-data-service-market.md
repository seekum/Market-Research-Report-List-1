<p><p>Automotive Data Service refers to the collection, analysis, and utilization of data generated by vehicles and their components. Firstly, the increasing adoption of connected cars and the rise of the Internet of Things (IoT) in the automotive industry have created vast opportunities for data collection and analysis.
