<p><p>Low Rolling Resistance Truck and Bus Radial Tire market is comprised of two main types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias tires are traditional tires constructed with crisscrossed layers of ply cords, providing flexibility and strength. Radial tires are more modern, with ply cords running radially from bead to bead, offering better fuel efficiency and longer tread life. While bias tires are cheaper, radial tires offer superior performance and are becoming increasingly popular in the commercial truck and bus market.</p></p>
