<p><p>Surface-mounted lamps are divided into three main types: Surface-Mounted Fluorescent, Surface-Mounted Halogen, and Other. Surface-Mounted Fluorescent lamps use fluorescent tubes to provide energy-efficient lighting, ideal for commercial and industrial spaces. Surface-Mounted Halogen lamps use halogen bulbs for a bright and focused light source, commonly used for accent lighting in residential settings. The Other category includes a variety of surface-mounted lamps using different technologies such as LED or incandescent bulbs, offering versatility in design and functionality for various lighting applications.</p></p>
