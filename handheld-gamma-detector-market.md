<p><strong>The Handheld Gamma Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CsI</li><li>NaI</li><li>LaBr</li><li>Geiger Muller</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld gamma detectors come in various types such as CsI, NaI, LaBr, Geiger Muller, and others. CsI detectors use cesium iodide crystals for gamma radiation detection, NaI detectors utilize sodium iodide crystals, and LaBr detectors use lanthanum bromide crystals. Geiger Muller detectors work by measuring ionizing radiation, while other types of handheld gamma detectors can include scintillation detectors with different crystal materials. Each type of detector offers specific advantages and limitations in detecting and measuring gamma radiation.</p></p>
