<p><p>The Automotive Aftermarket for Spark Plugs can be divided into two main market types based on their heat range - Hot Spark Plugs and Cold Spark Plugs. Hot Spark Plugs are designed to retain more heat, making them ideal for vehicles with high-speed, high-performance engines. Conversely, Cold Spark Plugs are designed to dissipate heat quickly, making them suitable for vehicles with lower-speed engines or that are subjected to heavy loads. Both types serve different purposes and cater to the specific needs of different vehicle engines.</p></p>
