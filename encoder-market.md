<p><strong>The Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Encoder</li><li>Linear Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Encoders are devices used to convert motion or position into a signal for communication with control systems. The two main types of encoders are rotary encoders and linear encoders. Rotary encoders measure rotation and angular position, while linear encoders measure linear motion along a path. Both types are essential in various industries including manufacturing, robotics, and automation. Rotary encoders are typically used in applications such as motors and robotics, while linear encoders are commonly found in CNC machines and precision measurement equipment.</p></p>
