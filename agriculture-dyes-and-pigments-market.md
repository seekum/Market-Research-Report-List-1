<p><p>Agriculture dyes are water-soluble colorants used to impart color to various agricultural products such as fertilizers, seeds, and pesticides. These dyes are often used for marking purposes in the agricultural industry. Pigments, on the other hand, are insoluble colorants that are used to provide long-lasting and vibrant colors to agricultural products. They are commonly used in coatings, paints, and inks. Both dyes and pigments play a crucial role in enhancing the visual appeal and functionality of agricultural products.</p></p>
