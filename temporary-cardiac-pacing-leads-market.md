<p><p>Temporary cardiac pacing leads are used in emergency situations to stabilize cardiac function. They are available in two types - Unipolar and Bipolar. Unipolar leads have one electrode which delivers electrical impulses to the heart, while Bipolar leads have two electrodes in close proximity which reduces the risk of myocardial damage. Both types are used based on the patient's condition and the desired outcome of pacing. They are essential tools in managing cardiac arrhythmias and ensuring proper heart functioning.</p></p>
