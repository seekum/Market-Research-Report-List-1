<p><p>Window Mode Watchdog Timer and Timeout Mode Watchdog Timer are two types of Automotive Watchdog Timers used in vehicles. Window Mode Watchdog Timer monitors the timing of specific events within a predefined window, resetting the timer if the events occur within the window. Timeout Mode Watchdog Timer monitors the timing of events and triggers a reset if they do not occur within a specified time frame. Both timers are critical in ensuring the proper functioning and safety of vehicle systems.</p></p>
