<p><strong>The Automotive Intelligent Headlight Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adaptive Front-lighting System</li><li>Adaptive Driving Beam System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Intelligent Headlight market consists of two main types: Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS) and Adaptive Driving Beam System (ADB). AFS adjusts the direction and intensity of the headlights based on factors such as vehicle speed, steering angle, and weather conditions to optimize visibility. ADB goes a step further by using sensors to detect oncoming vehicles and dynamically adjusting the headlight pattern to prevent glaring while maintaining maximum illumination. These advanced systems enhance driving safety by providing better visibility and reducing glare for both the driver and other road users.</p></p>
