<p><p>Insulated conduit is available in different types such as rock wool, fiberglass, calcium silicate, and others. Rock wool insulated conduit is made from natural stone and offers excellent thermal insulation. Fiberglass insulated conduit is made of glass fibers and is known for its light weight and durability. Calcium silicate insulated conduit is made from calcium silicate powder and provides good fire resistance. Other types of insulated conduit may include materials like foam, perlite, or vermiculite, each offering unique qualities for specific applications.</p></p>
