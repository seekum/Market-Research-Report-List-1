<p><p>PET Non-Woven Fabric Market includes various types such as Polyester Spunbond Nonwoven Fabric, which is made by spinning polyester fibers into a web-like structure. Polyester Needle Punched Nonwovens are created by entangling fibers through mechanical needle punching. Polyester Spunlace Nonwoven Fabric is manufactured by hydroentangling polyester fibers to form a strong fabric. Other types of PET Non-Woven Fabric may include thermal-bonded fabrics, air-laid fabrics, and meltblown fabrics. Each type offers unique characteristics and applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and healthcare.</p></p>
