<p><strong>The Automotive UV Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UVA</li><li>UVB</li><li>UVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive UV Sensors Market Types include UVA, UVB, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors measure long-wave ultraviolet radiation, primarily from the sun, which can penetrate the skin and cause long-term damage. UVB sensors measure medium-wave ultraviolet radiation, responsible for sunburn and skin cancer. UVC sensors detect short-wave ultraviolet radiation, typically filtered by the Earth's atmosphere but can be harmful in high concentrations. These sensors play a crucial role in automotive applications by monitoring UV exposure and alerting drivers to potential health risks.</p></p>
