Vinyl tiles are individual tiles made from PVC, while Luxury vinyl tiles are high-end, stylish options for a more upscale look. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of design, durability, and ease of maintenance, catering to different preferences and needs in the flooring market.</p></p>
