<p><p>Handguns market can be broadly classified into three main types based on their mechanism of operation: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic handguns are capable of firing continuously as long as the trigger is held down. Semi-automatic handguns require the shooter to manually chamber a round before each shot, but the firearm will automatically reload the next round. Manual handguns, on the other hand, require the shooter to manually chamber each round and eject spent casings after each shot. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of gun owners.</p></p>
