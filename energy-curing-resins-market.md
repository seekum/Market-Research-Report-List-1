<p><p>Energy curing resins are classified into two main types: Radical Type and Cationic Type. Radical Type resins use free radicals to initiate polymerization, making them suitable for UV and LED curing applications. On the other hand, Cationic Type resins use cations to initiate the curing process, offering advantages such as better adhesion and chemical resistance. Both types of resins are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and packaging due to their fast curing times and environmental benefits.</p></p>
