<p><p>Condensed type silicone electronic potting compound refers to a type of compound that undergoes a condensed reaction during curing, resulting in a rigid and durable material. Addition type silicone electronic potting compound, on the other hand, undergoes an addition reaction during curing, leading to a more flexible and elastic material. Both types of compounds are commonly used in electronic applications for encapsulation and protection of sensitive components. The choice between condensed and addition types depends on the specific requirements of the application, such as flexibility, durability, and thermal conductivity.</p></p>
