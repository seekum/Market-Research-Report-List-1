<p><p>Trivalent Chromium Finishing market includes three main types: plating, conversion coatings, and passivation. Plating involves depositing a layer of trivalent chromium onto a surface for enhanced durability and corrosion resistance. Conversion coatings use trivalent chromium to convert the metal surface into a protective layer. Passivation of a metal surface involves treating it with a solution containing trivalent chromium to improve its resistance to corrosion. Each type of trivalent chromium finishing offers unique benefits and applications in different industries.</p></p>
