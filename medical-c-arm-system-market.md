<p><p>Medical C-Arm Systems are essential imaging devices used in various medical procedures for real-time visualization of the patient's anatomy. There are two main types of C-arm systems - CCD Mobile C-arm and FPD Mobile C-arm. CCD Mobile C-arm systems use Charge-Coupled Device technology for image capture, while FPD Mobile C-arm systems use Flat Panel Detectors. Both systems offer high-quality imaging capabilities, but FPD C-arms generally provide better image quality and lower radiation dose to patients and medical personnel.</p></p>
