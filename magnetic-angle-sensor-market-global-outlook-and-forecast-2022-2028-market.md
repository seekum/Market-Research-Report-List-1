<p><p>Magnetic angle sensors are devices used to measure the angle or position of a magnetic field. The market is divided into three main types: TMR sensors, AMR sensors, and GMR sensors. TMR sensors utilize tunneling magnetoresistance technology, AMR sensors use anisotropic magnetoresistance, and GMR sensors rely on giant magnetoresistance. These sensors find applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics.
