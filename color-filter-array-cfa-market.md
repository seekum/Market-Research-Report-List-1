<p><p>Color Filter Array (CFA) is used in digital imaging sensors to capture color information in images. The most common types of CFA in the market include the Bayer Filter, RGBE Filter, RYYB Filter, and Others. The Bayer Filter is the most widely used CFA, dividing pixels into red, green, and blue filters to create a color image. The RGBE Filter adds an additional filter for enhanced image quality, while the RYYB Filter uses a yellow filter to improve low-light performance. Other CFAs on the market offer different combinations of color filters for various applications.</p></p>
