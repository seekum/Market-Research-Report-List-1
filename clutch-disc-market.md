<p><strong>The Clutch Disc Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Clutch</li><li>Electromagnetic Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clutch discs are essential components in both friction clutches and electromagnetic clutches. In friction clutches, the clutch disc helps transfer power from the engine to the transmission by creating friction between the disc and the pressure plate. On the other hand, in electromagnetic clutches, the clutch disc is used to engage and disengage the clutch by activating an electromagnetic coil. Both types of clutches rely on the clutch disc to effectively control the power transfer in various applications.</p></p>
