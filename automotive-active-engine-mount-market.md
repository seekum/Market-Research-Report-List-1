<p><strong>The Automotive Active Engine Mount Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-active Engine Mount</li><li>Active Engine Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Active Engine Mount Market includes Semi-active Engine Mounts and Active Engine Mounts. Semi-active Engine Mounts use a combination of hydraulic fluid and electronic sensors to adjust stiffness based on driving conditions. Active Engine Mounts use electronic sensors and actuators to constantly adjust stiffness, offering precise control over vibrations. Semi-active Engine Mounts are more cost-effective than Active Engine Mounts but provide less control. Active Engine Mounts offer superior vibration control but are more expensive. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing comfort and performance in vehicles.</p></p>
