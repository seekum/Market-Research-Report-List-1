<p><strong>The Electro-deposited Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultra-thin Copper Foil (thickness below 9 μm)</li><li>General Copper Foil (thickness above 9 μm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-deposited copper foil can be categorized into two main types based on thickness: Ultra-thin copper foil, which refers to foils with a thickness below 9 μm, and general copper foil, which includes foils with a thickness above 9 μm. Ultra-thin copper foil is preferred for applications where flexibility and weight are crucial, such as in flexible printed circuits. On the other hand, general copper foil is commonly used in applications like lithium-ion batteries, electromagnetic interference shielding, and printed circuit boards.</p></p>
