<p><p>C4 LLDPE film is used in three main market types: Blown Film Extrusion, Cast Film Extrusion, and Others. Blown film extrusion involves the process of blowing air into the film bubble to expand it, creating a thinner and stronger film. Cast film extrusion is formed by cooling the film on a chill roll, resulting in a smoother and more consistent film. The "Others" market includes various applications such as stretch wrapping, protective packaging, and agricultural films.</p></p>
