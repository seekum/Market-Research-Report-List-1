<p><p>Iron and Steel Slag market can be classified into different types based on the production methods. The Hot Method involves pouring molten slag into water or other cooling medium to solidify and produce granulated slag. The Hot Boring Method uses high-pressure water jets to disintegrate the slag, creating small particles. The Roller Method involves crushing the cooled slag using rollers to produce fine particles. Other methods include air cooling or using chemicals to granulate the slag for various industrial applications.</p></p>
