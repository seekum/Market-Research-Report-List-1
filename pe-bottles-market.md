<p><p>PE bottles market consists of three main types: HDPE (high-density polyethylene), LDPE (low-density polyethylene), and MDPE (medium-density polyethylene). HDPE bottles are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for packaging liquids such as detergents and chemicals. LDPE bottles are flexible and great for squeeze bottles or bags. MDPE bottles offer a balance between the strength of HDPE and the flexibility of LDPE, making them suitable for various packaging applications. Each type caters to different needs in the packaging industry.</p></p>
