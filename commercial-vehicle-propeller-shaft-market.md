<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Propeller Shaft market is divided into two types - Rigid Shaft and Hollow Shaft. Rigid Shafts are solid and provide high torque transmission with minimal vibration. They are widely used in heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Hollow Shafts are lighter and have lower inertia, making them more suitable for vehicles requiring high speeds and efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in transmitting power from the engine to the wheels in commercial vehicles, ensuring smooth and efficient operation.</p></p>
