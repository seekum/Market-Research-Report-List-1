<p><ul><li>Cloud-based Self-Organising Networks</li><li>On-premise Self-Organising Networks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-organising network (SON) market can be divided into two main types: cloud-based SON and on-premise SON. Cloud-based SON is a network management approach where the SON functionalities are hosted and delivered through cloud infrastructure. This allows for more flexibility and scalability as the network can be easily accessed from anywhere. On the other hand, on-premise SON refers to the deployment of SON functionalities within the physical network infrastructure, giving the network operator more control and security but requiring dedicated hardware and maintenance.</p></p>
