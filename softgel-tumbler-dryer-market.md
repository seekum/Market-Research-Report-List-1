<p><p>Standard Tumble Drying Systems are designed for batch processing and involve placing softgel capsules in a tumbler dryer for a specified amount of time to remove moisture. Continuous Tumble Drying Systems, on the other hand, are designed for continuous production and involve a conveyor system that moves softgel capsules through the drying process without the need for batch processing. Both types of systems are used in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries to ensure softgel capsules are properly dried before packaging.</p></p>
