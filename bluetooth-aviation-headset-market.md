<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bluetooth Aviation Headset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) Headsets</li><li>Active Noise Reduction (ANR) Headsets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) Headsets in the Bluetooth Aviation Headset market are designed to block out external noise through soundproofing materials, offering a basic level of noise reduction for pilots. On the other hand, Active Noise Reduction (ANR) Headsets use advanced technology to actively cancel out unwanted noise, providing a higher level of noise reduction and enhanced clarity for communication. ANR headsets are more expensive than PNR headsets but offer superior noise-cancelling capabilities, making them a popular choice among professional pilots.</p></p>
