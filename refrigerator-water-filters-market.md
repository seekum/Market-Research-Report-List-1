<p><strong>The Refrigerator Water Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters</li><li>Carbon Block Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refrigerator water filters come in two main types: Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters and Carbon Block Filters. GAC filters consist of loose carbon granules that trap contaminants in the water, while carbon block filters contain compressed carbon blocks that provide more effective filtration. GAC filters are typically more affordable but have a shorter lifespan, while carbon block filters are more durable and provide better filtration. Both types are effective at removing impurities and improving the taste and quality of drinking water.</p></p>
