<p><p>Garbage collection trucks come in three main types: Front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders are equipped with a hydraulic arm in the front to pick up and empty large trash bins. Rear loaders feature a compacting mechanism in the back to crush and store trash efficiently. Side loaders have an opening on the side for workers to manually load garbage into the truck. Each type of truck is suited for different collection needs and preferences.</p></p>
