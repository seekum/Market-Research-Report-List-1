<p><p>The XLPE market can be classified into three types based on the cross-linking method used - silane cross-linked, peroxide cross-linked, and radiation cross-linked. Silane cross-linked XLPE is produced using silane compounds, peroxide cross-linked XLPE uses peroxide chemicals, and radiation cross-linked XLPE is manufactured with the help of radiation. Each type offers unique properties and benefits, making them suitable for various applications in industries such as power cables, automotive, and construction. Overall, these different cross-linking methods play a crucial role in determining the characteristics of XLPE products.</p></p>
