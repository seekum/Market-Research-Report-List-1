<p><ul><li>AC/DC</li><li>Isolated DC/DC</li><li>Sequencers</li><li>DC/AC</li><li>Hot Swap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital power conversion market includes various types such as AC/DC, Isolated DC/DC, Sequencers, DC/AC, and Hot Swap. AC/DC converters are used to convert alternating current to direct current, while isolated DC/DC converters provide galvanic isolation between input and output. Sequencers help in controlling the timing of power supply turn-on/off sequences. DC/AC converters are used for converting direct current to alternating current, and hot swap devices allow for replacing components while the system is still running. This market offers a range of solutions for efficient power conversion in different applications.</p></p>
