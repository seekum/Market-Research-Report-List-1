<p><p>The Automotive Auto Dimming Mirror market consists of two main types: Outer Auto Dimming Mirrors and Inside Auto Dimming Mirrors. Outer Auto Dimming Mirrors are typically located on the exterior of the vehicle and automatically adjust to reduce glare from headlights behind the car. Inside Auto Dimming Mirrors are located inside the vehicle and function in a similar way, adjusting to reduce glare from headlights without the driver needing to manually adjust the mirror. Both types of mirrors enhance driving visibility and safety in varying lighting conditions.</p></p>
