<p><p>Release liner labels are a type of letterpress printing self-adhesive label that includes a liner or backing paper on the underside of the label. These labels are commonly used for applications where the labels need to be peeled off easily and applied to different surfaces. On the other hand, liner-less labels do not have a backing paper, making them more eco-friendly as they eliminate the need for liner waste. Both types of labels are widely used in various industries for branding, packaging, and information purposes.</p></p>
