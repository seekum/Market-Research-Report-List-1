<p><strong>The Railway AC Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized</li><li>Local Centralized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centralized market for railway AC units involves a single centralized cooling system that cools the entire train or railway car. This system allows for precise control of temperature and humidity levels throughout the entire train, ensuring passenger comfort. Local centralized systems, on the other hand, install separate cooling units in different areas of the train or railway car. While still centrally controlled, this type of system allows for more flexibility in controlling the climate in different sections of the train.</p></p>
