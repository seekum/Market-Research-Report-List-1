<p><p>Radial tires and bias tires are two main types of tires available in the car tire market. Radial tires have cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better grip, fuel efficiency, and overall performance compared to bias tires. Bias tires have cords that run at an angle, providing more stability and durability, especially in heavy-duty applications. Both types have their advantages and are suitable for different types of vehicles, terrains, and driving conditions.</p></p>
