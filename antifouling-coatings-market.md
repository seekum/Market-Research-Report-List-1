<p><p>Antifouling coatings are categorized into various types based on their properties and ingredients. Self-polishing antifouling coatings release biocides gradually, preventing marine organisms from attaching to the surface. Copper-free antifouling coatings use alternative environmentally friendly substances to inhibit fouling. Nano antifouling coatings utilize nano-size particles to create a smooth surface that repels organisms. Other types of antifouling coatings may include silicone-based or hybrid coatings that offer unique fouling resistance properties. Each type of coating serves to protect marine surfaces from biofouling in different ways.</p></p>
