<p><p>The soft tissue suture anchors market consists of different types of anchors used in surgeries. Metallic suture anchors are made of metal alloys, providing high strength and stability. PEEK suture anchors are made of a biocompatible polymer, offering good mechanical properties and radiolucency. Bio-composite suture anchors are composed of a combination of biodegradable materials and synthetic polymers, promoting tissue integration and reducing inflammation. Other types of suture anchors may include absorbable anchors or knotless anchors. These anchors provide various options for surgeons to choose from based on their specific surgical needs.</p></p>
