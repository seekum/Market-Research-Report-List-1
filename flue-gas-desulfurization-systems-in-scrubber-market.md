<p><p>Flue-gas desulfurization systems in scrubber market types can be classified as Type I and Type II. Type I systems typically involve wet scrubbers that use a lime or limestone slurry to remove sulfur dioxide from flue gas. These systems are effective in removing a high percentage of sulfur pollutants. On the other hand, Type II systems utilize dry scrubbing technology with sorbent injection to achieve sulfur dioxide removal. Type II systems are often more cost-effective and may be preferred in certain industrial applications.</p></p>
