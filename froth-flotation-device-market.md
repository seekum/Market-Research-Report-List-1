<p><strong>The Froth Flotation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell-to-Cell Flotation</li><li>Free-Flow Flotation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Froth flotation devices are used to separate valuable minerals from gangue through a process where air bubbles are introduced into a mixture of finely ground ore and water. The market for froth flotation devices can be divided into two main types: cell-to-cell flotation and free-flow flotation. In cell-to-cell flotation, the cells are interconnected to increase efficiency and reduce energy consumption. In free-flow flotation, the cells are not connected, allowing for more flexibility in the process. Both types are widely used in the mining industry for mineral processing.</p></p>
