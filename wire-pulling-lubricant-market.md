<p><p>The wire-pulling lubricant market is segmented into three types: liquid, wax, and gel. Liquid lubricants are easy to apply and spread evenly on wires, providing smooth pulling. Wax lubricants are solid at room temperature but melt when heated, offering enhanced lubrication for tough wire-pulling tasks. Gel lubricants are thick and viscous, designed for vertical applications to prevent dripping or running. Each type caters to different wire-pulling needs, providing varying levels of lubrication and ease of use.</p></p>
