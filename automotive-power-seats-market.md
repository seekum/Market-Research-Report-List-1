<p><p>The Automotive Power Seats Market can be divided into three main types based on the number of motors used in them: Single Motor Type, Double Motor Type, and Others. Single Motor Type power seats have one motor that controls both the seat back and seat bottom movements. Double Motor Type power seats have two motors, where one controls the seat back movement and the other controls the seat bottom movement independently. The Others category includes power seats with unique features or multiple motors for additional functionalities.</p></p>
