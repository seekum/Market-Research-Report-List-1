<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Digital Mass Flow Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Pressure Type</li><li>Differential Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital mass flow controllers are commonly classified into two main types: thermal pressure type and differential pressure type. </p><p>Thermal pressure type mass flow controllers operate by measuring the heat transfer from a heated sensor to the flowing gas, providing an accurate measurement of mass flow. </p><p>On the other hand, differential pressure type mass flow controllers measure the pressure drop across a restriction in the flow path to determine the flow rate. </p><p>Both types offer precise control and monitoring of gas flow in a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
