<p><p>Electrocardiography devices and accessories are used to monitor and diagnose heart conditions. The market types are categorized based on the number of tracks that can be recorded simultaneously. Single-track devices can record one lead at a time, limiting the information captured. Three-way devices can record three leads at the same time, providing a broader perspective. Six-way devices can record six leads simultaneously, offering more comprehensive data. Twelve-way devices can record twelve leads, enabling a detailed analysis of the heart's electrical activity. Each market type offers different levels of monitoring capabilities for improved cardiac assessment.</p></p>
