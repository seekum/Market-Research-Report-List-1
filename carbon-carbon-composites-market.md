<p><p>Carbon carbon composites market consist of two main types of manufacturing processes: Chemical Vapor Deposition and Liquid Impregnation Process. </p><p>Chemical Vapor Deposition involves the deposition of carbon atoms on a substrate in a gaseous state, leading to strong and lightweight composites with excellent mechanical properties.</p><p>On the other hand, Liquid Impregnation Process involves impregnating carbon fabric with a resin that is solidified, resulting in composites with high strength and toughness.</p><p>Both processes are widely used in aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods industries.</p></p>
