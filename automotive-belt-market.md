<p><strong>The Automotive Belt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Timing Belts</li><li>Drive Belts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Timing belts and drive belts are two main types of automotive belts used in vehicles. Timing belts are designed to synchronize the rotation of the crankshaft and camshaft, ensuring proper timing of engine valves. Drive belts, also known as serpentine belts, transmit power from the engine to various components like the alternator, water pump, and air conditioning compressor. Both types of belts play a crucial role in the proper functioning of a vehicle's engine and overall performance.</p></p>
