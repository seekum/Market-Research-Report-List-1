<p><p>Magnetic proximity sensors are classified into two main types: Fixed Distance Sensors and Adjustable Distance Sensors. Fixed Distance Sensors are set to detect magnetic fields at a specific distance and cannot be changed. On the other hand, Adjustable Distance Sensors can be manually adjusted to detect magnetic fields at different distances. Both types of sensors are commonly used in industrial automation, automotive, and consumer electronics industries for tasks such as object detection, positioning, and speed monitoring.</p></p>
