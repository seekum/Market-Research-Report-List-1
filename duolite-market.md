<p><p>Duolite offers market types for cationic resins and anionic resins. Cationic resins are positively charged ion exchange resins that selectively remove negatively charged ions from water or other solutions. They are commonly used in industries such as water treatment and wastewater management. On the other hand, anionic resins are negatively charged ion exchange resins that remove positively charged ions from solutions. They are also widely used in various industrial processes for purification and separation purposes.</p></p>
