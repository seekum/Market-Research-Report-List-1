<p><p>Real-time Location System (RTLS) is a technology that enables tracking and monitoring of objects or people in real-time. There are several types of RTLS, including RFID, Wi-Fi, Ultrasound, Infrared, and Zigbee. </p><p>RFID uses radio frequency identification to track assets or individuals by attaching RFID tags to them. Wi-Fi RTLS utilizes Wi-Fi signals to locate objects or people within a designated area. Ultrasound RTLS uses soundwaves to determine the location of objects or individuals. Infrared RTLS relies on infrared signals to track and monitor movement. Zigbee RTLS utilizes Zigbee wireless technology to enable precise location tracking.</p></p>
