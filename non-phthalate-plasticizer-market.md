<p><p>Non-phthalate plasticizers are alternative additives used in plastic materials to improve flexibility and durability without containing harmful phthalates. Adipates, trimellitates, epoxies, and benzoates are different types of non-phthalate plasticizers available in the market. Adipates are widely used due to their excellent low-temperature performance and compatibility with various polymers. Trimellitates provide high-temperature resistance and are suitable for automotive and wire applications. Epoxies offer excellent heat and stain resistance, making them ideal for flooring and wire coatings. Benzoates are known for their high solvating power and are often used in adhesives and sealants.</p></p>
