<p><p>ZigBee enabled lighting offers two market types: ZigBee lamps and ZigBee luminaires. ZigBee lamps refer to individual light bulbs that can be wirelessly controlled using ZigBee technology. These lamps can be easily integrated into existing lighting systems, allowing users to adjust brightness, color, and other settings remotely. On the other hand, ZigBee luminaires encompass complete lighting fixtures, such as ceiling lights or wall sconces, which are equipped with built-in ZigBee capability. These luminaires offer more advanced features, including synchronized lighting effects, group control, and energy management.</p></p>
