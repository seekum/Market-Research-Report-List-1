<p><p>Digital I-O modules are devices used for input and output operations in industrial automation systems. Wired Digital I-O modules utilize physical wires to transmit data between devices, offering a stable and secure connection. On the other hand, Wireless Digital I-O modules use wireless communication protocols like Wi-Fi or Bluetooth to transmit data without the need for physical wires, providing greater flexibility and convenience in setup and operation. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for industrial control systems.</p></p>
