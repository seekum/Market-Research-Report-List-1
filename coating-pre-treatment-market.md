<p><p>Coating pre-treatment market includes four main types: phosphate coating pre-treatment, chromate coating pre-treatment, blast clean coating pre-treatment, and chromate-free coating pre-treatment. Phosphate coating pre-treatment is used to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance. Chromate coating pre-treatment provides corrosion protection and promotes paint adhesion. Blast clean coating pre-treatment involves using abrasive materials to clean and roughen the substrate surface. Chromate-free coating pre-treatment is an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional chromate coatings, offering similar benefits without the use of harmful chemicals.</p></p>
