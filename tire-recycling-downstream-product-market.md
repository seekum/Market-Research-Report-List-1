<p><p>Tire recycling downstream product market types include Pyrolysis Recycling Technique and Shredding Recycling Technique. Pyrolysis recycling involves heating tires to high temperatures in an oxygen-free environment, resulting in the production of oil, gas, and carbon black which can be used as fuels or raw materials. Shredding recycling technique, on the other hand, involves shredding tires into small pieces which can be used as raw materials for various applications like construction, playground surfaces, and rubberized asphalt. These techniques help in the sustainable management of tire waste and promote circular economy principles.</p></p>
