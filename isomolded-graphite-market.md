<p><strong>The Isomolded Graphite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CIP Method</li><li>Vibration Molding Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isomolded Graphite Market includes two main types based on manufacturing methods: CIP method and Vibration Molding Method. The CIP method involves compressing the graphite powder into a mold under high pressure to produce a solid shape. On the other hand, Vibration Molding Method involves vibrating the graphite powder in a mold to eliminate air pockets and ensure a dense final product. Both methods are used in the production of high-quality isomolded graphite products for various industrial applications.</p></p>
