<p><strong>The MDI Based Polyurethane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>MDI Based Polyurethane Market consists of two main types, Pure MDI and Polymeric MDI (PMDI). Pure MDI is a high-quality, highly reactive chemical used in the production of rigid polyurethane foams, coatings, and adhesives. PMDI, on the other hand, is a less reactive form of MDI that is widely used in flexible polyurethane foam applications. Both Pure MDI and PMDI play a crucial role in the global polyurethane market, offering different properties and applications based on their reactivity and chemical composition.</p></p>
