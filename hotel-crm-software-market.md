<p><strong>The Hotel CRM Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>Premise Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hotel CRM software is available in two market types: cloud-based and premise-based. Cloud-based software is hosted on third-party servers and accessible over the internet, allowing hotels to access their data from any device with an internet connection. It offers flexibility, scalability, and requires minimal hardware and maintenance costs. On the other hand, premise-based software is installed on a hotel's own servers and managed internally. It offers more control over data and security but requires upfront investment, ongoing maintenance, and limited accessibility.</p></p>
