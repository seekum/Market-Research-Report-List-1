Foils Below 20 μm are used in applications requiring extremely thin foils, such as flexible electronics. Foils in the 20-50 μm range are commonly used in printed circuit boards and high-frequency applications. Foils Above 50 μm are typically used in power electronics and automotive applications. Each type caters to specific industry requirements based on the thickness of the copper foil needed.</p></p>
