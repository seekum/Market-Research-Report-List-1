<p><p>Agricultural dehumidifiers can be classified into two main types: Mobile and Stationary. Stationary dehumidifiers, on the other hand, are built to stay in one place and are generally used in larger agricultural facilities. Both types of dehumidifiers are essential for controlling moisture levels in agricultural settings to prevent mold growth, ensure proper air circulation, and maintain optimal growing conditions for crops.</p></p>
