<p><p>The continuous flow apheresis device market comprises various types of devices, including plasma separators, plasma component separators, immunoadsorption columns, plasma perfusion columns, and hemoperfusion columns. Plasma separators are used to separate plasma from other blood components, while plasma component separators separate specific plasma components. Immunoadsorption columns remove specific substances from the blood, and plasma perfusion columns remove plasma and pass it through an adsorbent material. Hemoperfusion columns remove toxins from the blood. These devices are essential in various medical procedures, such as therapeutic plasma exchange and liver support therapy.</p></p>
