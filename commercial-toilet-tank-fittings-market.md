<p><ul><li>Fill Valve</li><li>Flush Valve</li><li>Push Button & Lever</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial toilet tank fittings market can be categorized into four types: fill valve, flush valve, push button & lever, and others. Fill valves regulate the water flow into the tank, while flush valves control the release of water during flushing. Push button & lever mechanisms are used to trigger the flushing process. The others category may include components such as flappers, trip levers, and gaskets. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of commercial toilet tanks.</p></p>
