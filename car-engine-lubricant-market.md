<p><p>The car engine lubricant market is primarily divided into three types: Conventional Oil, Full-synthetic Oil, and Synthetic-blend Oil. Conventional Oil is the most commonly used and affordable option, containing basic additives and mineral oil. Full-synthetic Oil is more expensive but offers superior performance, longevity, and protection due to its pure synthetic composition. Synthetic-blend Oil combines the benefits of both conventional and full-synthetic oils, providing a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of consumers in the market.</p></p>
