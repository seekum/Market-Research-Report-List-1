<p><p>Cemented carbide plates come in two main types based on the size of the tungsten carbide grains used in their production. Coarse grain WC plates are known for their higher toughness and impact resistance, making them suitable for applications requiring strength and durability. On the other hand, fine grain WC plates offer improved wear resistance and hardness, making them ideal for precision cutting and machining tasks. Both types offer unique benefits depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
