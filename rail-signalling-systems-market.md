<p><p>Rail Signalling Systems market includes two main types: Traditional Train Control and Communication Based Train Control (CBTC). Traditional Train Control utilizes trackside signalling equipment, such as signals, switches, and track circuits, to control train movement and ensure safe operation. On the other hand, CBTC utilizes advanced communication technologies to enable direct communication between trains and the control center, allowing for real-time train control and better coordination. CBTC offers enhanced capacity, efficiency, and safety compared to Traditional Train Control systems.</p></p>
