<p><p>Metastatic cancer treatment drug market can be classified into two main types: chemotherapy drugs and targeted therapy drugs. Chemotherapy drugs are designed to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells throughout the body, often causing side effects in healthy cells. On the other hand, targeted therapy drugs are designed to target specific molecular alterations in cancer cells, hence reducing harm to normal cells and often resulting in more effective treatment. These two types of drugs play crucial roles in the treatment of metastatic cancer, offering different approaches to combat the disease.</p></p>
