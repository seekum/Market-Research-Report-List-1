<p><p>Vacuum On-load Tap Changer (OLTC) market includes two main types: single-phase and three-phase. Single-phase OLTCs are designed for transformers that have single-phase windings, making them suitable for applications where voltage regulation is needed for individual phases. On the other hand, three-phase OLTCs are used with transformers that have three-phase windings, providing voltage regulation for the entire system. Both types of OLTCs are crucial for maintaining the stability and efficiency of power distribution systems.</p></p>
