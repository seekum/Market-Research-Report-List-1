<p><strong>The Intensive Care Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated</li><li>Non-integrated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intensive Care Software Market can be divided into two types: Integrated and Non-integrated. </p><p>Integrated software refers to a comprehensive solution that is designed to seamlessly integrate with other systems in a healthcare facility. It offers functionalities such as patient management, medical records, and care planning. This type of software enables healthcare professionals to access and update patient data in real-time.</p><p>On the other hand, non-integrated software operates as standalone applications that do not communicate with other systems in the facility. It typically provides specific functionalities, such as bedside monitoring or ventilator management. Although non-integrated software may perform specialized tasks, it lacks the interoperability and comprehensive features found in integrated software.</p></p>
