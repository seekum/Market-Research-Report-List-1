<p><p>Aerospace electrically conductive sealants are used in the aerospace industry to provide electrical conductivity and protection against electromagnetic interference. Three common types of these sealants are nickel filled sealants, silver filled sealants, and copper filled sealants. Nickel filled sealants have high strength and resistance to corrosion. Silver filled sealants offer the highest level of electrical conductivity. Copper filled sealants have good thermal conductivity and are suitable for applications requiring high electrical conductivity. These sealants play a crucial role in ensuring the performance and safety of aerospace components.</p></p>
