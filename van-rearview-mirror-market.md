<p><p>The van rearview mirror market includes three main types: exterior mirrors, interior mirrors, and under rearview mirrors. Exterior mirrors are typically mounted on the sides of the van and provide visibility to the driver of surrounding traffic. Interior mirrors are located inside the van and are used to monitor passengers and cargo in the rear of the vehicle. Under rearview mirrors are mounted underneath the van and help drivers see obstacles and objects close to the ground. Each type serves a different purpose in enhancing driver visibility and safety.</p></p>
