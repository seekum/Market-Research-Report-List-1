<p><p>The Data Center Solution market can be classified into two main types: Electrical Data Center Solutions and Mechanical Data Center Solutions. </p><p>Electrical Data Center Solutions focus on providing efficient and reliable electrical infrastructure, such as power supply, distribution, and backup systems, to ensure uninterrupted operations of data centers. These solutions aim to optimize power usage and minimize downtime.</p><p>Mechanical Data Center Solutions, on the other hand, primarily deal with the cooling and ventilation systems within data centers. They focus on regulating temperature, humidity, and air quality to maintain optimal conditions for the servers and networking equipment, preventing overheating and potential damage.</p><p>Both types of solutions are crucial for the smooth functioning and sustainability of data centers.</p></p>
