<p><p>The Instant Adhesive Gel market includes two major types: Cyanoacrylate Instant Adhesives and Epoxy-based Instant Adhesives. Cyanoacrylate Instant Adhesives are known for their fast bonding capability on various surfaces and their resistance to temperature and moisture. They are commonly used for bonding plastics, rubber, and metals. On the other hand, Epoxy-based Instant Adhesives offer high strength bonding, resistance to chemicals, and versatility in applications. They are often utilized for bonding wood, ceramics, glass, and metal substrates. Both types of instant adhesives have significant demand in various industries.</p></p>
