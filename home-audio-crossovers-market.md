<p><strong>The Home Audio Crossovers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home audio crossovers are devices used in sound systems to separate audio frequencies and distribute them to the correct speakers. Active crossovers are built with integrated amplifiers and require a power source to operate. They offer more control over sound quality, allowing adjustments to be made for each speaker. Passive crossovers, on the other hand, do not require power and are built into the speakers themselves. They are simpler and more cost-effective, but have less flexibility in sound customization. Both types cater to different customer preferences and budget considerations.</p></p>
