<p><strong>The Robotic Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crawler Drive robotic pool cleaners use tracks or treads to move across the pool surface, providing better traction and maneuverability especially for inclines and walls. They are well-suited for larger pools with complex shapes. On the other hand, Wheel Drive robotic pool cleaners use wheels to navigate the pool, offering faster speeds and better performance on flat surfaces. They are suitable for smaller pools with simpler layouts. Both types offer efficient cleaning and convenience, catering to different pool sizes and shapes.</p></p>
