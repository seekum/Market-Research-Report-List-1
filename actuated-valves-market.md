<p><p>Actuated valves refer to valves that are operated or controlled by an external power source known as an actuator. There are three main types of actuated valves in the market - pneumatic actuators, electric actuators, and others. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to operate the valve, while electric actuators use electric power. The "others" category includes hydraulic actuators, manual actuators, and other less common types. Each type of actuated valve offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
