<p><p>The veterinary surgical equipment market includes various types of instruments used during veterinary surgeries. Forceps are used for grasping and holding tissues, while scalpels and surgical scissors are used for cutting and dissecting tissues. Hooks and retractors assist in tissue retraction and exposure. Trocars and cannulas allow access to body cavities. Electro-surgery instruments use electric current for cutting, coagulation, and desiccation. Other instruments in the market include staplers, needle holders, and bone surgery instruments.</p></p>
