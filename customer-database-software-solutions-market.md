<p><p>Customer database software solutions are available in two main market types: cloud deployment and on-premise. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency, as users can access the software from anywhere and don't need to manage servers themselves. On the other hand, on-premise solutions are installed and maintained locally on the company's own servers.
