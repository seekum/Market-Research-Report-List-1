<p><p>Oral thin film drug manufacturing is a process in which pharmaceutical drugs are formulated into thin films that are placed on the tongue for rapid dissolution and absorption. The rising prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cancer, diabetes, and cardiovascular diseases, is increasing the demand for innovative drug delivery systems. The convenience and ease of administration offered by oral thin films are driving their adoption among patients, especially children and the elderly.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology and the growing focus on research and development activities are fueling the market growth. Manufacturers are investing in the development of novel drug formulations and manufacturing techniques to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of oral thin films.</p><p>In addition, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the market growth as the demand for rapid and convenient drug delivery systems has increased.
