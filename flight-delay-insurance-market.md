<p><p>The active claims market in flight delay insurance refers to the policies where the insured party actively submits a claim to the insurance company in the event of a flight delay. The insurer reviews the claim and compensates the insured accordingly. On the other hand, the passive claims market involves insurance policies that automatically pay out a predetermined amount of compensation to the insured without the need for the insured to actively submit a claim. The insurer monitors flight delays and triggers the payment process automatically.</p></p>
