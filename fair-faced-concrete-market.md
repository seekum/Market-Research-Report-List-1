<p><p>Fair faced concrete is a type of concrete that is designed to be aesthetically pleasing and free of imperfections. There are three main market types for fair faced concrete: premix, precast, and others. Premix fair faced concrete is mixed on site and poured into molds to create the desired finish. Precast fair faced concrete is pre-made in a factory and then transported to the construction site for installation. Other market types may include sprayed concrete or thin layer applications for a smoother finish.</p></p>
