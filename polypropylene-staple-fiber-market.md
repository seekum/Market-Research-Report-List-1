<p><p>Polypropylene Staple Fiber is divided into two main types based on their water absorption properties. Hydrophobic Polypropylene Staple Fiber is water repellent and does not absorb moisture, making it ideal for applications requiring water resistance. On the other hand, Hydrophilic Polypropylene Staple Fiber absorbs water easily and is commonly used in products that require moisture management, such as textiles and hygiene products. These two market types cater to different needs and preferences in various industries.</p></p>
