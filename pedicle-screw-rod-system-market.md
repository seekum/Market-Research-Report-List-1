<p><strong>The Pedicle Screw Rod System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monoaxial Pedicle Screw Rod System</li><li>Polyaxial Pedicle Screw Rod System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pedicle screw rod systems are used in spinal surgeries to stabilize the spine. Monoaxial systems have screws that are fixed at a specific angle to the rod, allowing for more precise alignment but requiring more complex surgical techniques. Polyaxial systems have screws that can move in multiple directions, offering more flexibility during surgery. Both systems are important in treating conditions like scoliosis and spinal fractures, with surgeons choosing the appropriate system based on the individual patient's needs.</p></p>
