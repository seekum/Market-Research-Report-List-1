<p><p>Carpet and rugs market types include woven, tufted, knotted, and needle-punched varieties. Woven carpets are made on a loom with fibers woven together to create intricate patterns. Tufted carpets are made by pushing yarn through a backing material to create a textured surface. Knotted carpets involve hand-tying individual knots to create intricate designs. Needle-punched carpets are made by punching fibers through a backing material to create a dense and durable product. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits for consumers.</p></p>
