<p><p>Automotive Advanced Suspension Systems are classified into two types: Independent Automotive Suspension System and Non-independent Automotive Suspension System. Independent systems allow individual movement of each wheel, providing better handling and ride quality. Non-independent systems, on the other hand, connect the wheels on the same axle, leading to less flexibility and control over uneven terrain. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different preferences and needs of drivers in the automotive market.</p></p>
