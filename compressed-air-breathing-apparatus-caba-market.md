<p><strong>The Compressed Air Breathing Apparatus (CABA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit CABA</li><li>Closed-Circuit CABA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compressed Air Breathing Apparatus, or CABA, is utilized for supplying clean air to individuals working in hazardous environments. It comes in different types, including Open-Circuit CABA and Closed-Circuit CABA. Open-Circuit CABA introduces fresh air to the user and expels exhaled air, offering protection through a mask or hood. In contrast, Closed-Circuit CABA recirculates exhaled air, removing carbon dioxide and replenishing oxygen. These CABA systems cater to distinct market demands, where the selection depends on the specific requirements and conditions of the intended application.</p></p>
