<p><p>IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) and Thyristor are two important power semiconductor devices used in various applications like power supplies, motor drives, inverters, and UPS systems. IGBT is a three-terminal power semiconductor device that combines the advantages of both MOSFET and bipolar junction transistor, offering high switching speeds and low on-state voltage drops. Whereas, Thyristor is a four-layered semiconductor device that acts as a switch, controlling the flow of electricity in a circuit.</p><p>The IGBT and Thyristor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period.
