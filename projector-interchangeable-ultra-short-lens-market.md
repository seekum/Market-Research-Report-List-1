<p><p>Projector Interchangeable Ultra Short Lens market types include Straight, L-shaped (90°), Folding (not 90°, not 180°), and Folding (180°). Straight lenses are directly installed without any angles. L-shaped lenses are attached at a 90° angle for specific projector placements. Folding lenses (not 90°, not 180°) allow for flexible positioning without a complete 90° or 180° turn. Folding lenses (180°) enable projectors to be mounted upside down or in a vertical position for unique projection angles.</p></p>
