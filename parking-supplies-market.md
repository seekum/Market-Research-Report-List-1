<p><p>The parking supplies market offers various types of products to enhance parking safety and organization. Speed bumps are raised structures placed across roadways to slow down vehicles. Wheel stops are barriers used to prevent cars from rolling too far forward or backward. Speed humps are elongated rounded bumps designed to reduce vehicle speed. Speed cushions are similar to speed humps but have gaps to accommodate emergency vehicle tires. Other parking supplies may include parking barriers, signage, and delineators. These products collectively aim to improve traffic flow, prevent accidents, and enhance parking efficiency.</p></p>
