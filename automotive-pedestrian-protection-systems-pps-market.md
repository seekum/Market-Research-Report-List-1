<p><p>Pedestrian Protection Systems (PPS) in the automotive industry can be classified into two main types: Passive Pedestrian Protection System and Active Pedestrian Protection System. Passive systems include features like energy-absorbing bumpers and hoods that are designed to minimize injury in the event of a collision. Active systems, on the other hand, use sensors and cameras to detect pedestrians and automatically apply the brakes or initiate evasive maneuvers to avoid accidents. Both types of systems are designed to enhance pedestrian safety on the road.</p></p>
