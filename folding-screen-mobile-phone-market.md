<p><ul><li>Inner Fold</li><li>Out Fold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Folding screen mobile phones come in two main types: inner fold and out fold. Inner fold phones have a screen that folds inward, protecting it from scratches and damage when closed. This design also allows for a larger, more immersive display when the phone is unfolded. On the other hand, out fold phones have a screen that folds outward, providing a compact and pocket-friendly design when closed, while still offering a larger display when opened. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
