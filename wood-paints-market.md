<p><p>Wood paints market is segmented into two main types - Waterborne and Oil-based. Waterborne type paints are popular for their low odor, easy clean-up, and quick drying time. They are also environmentally friendly as they have low VOC content. On the other hand, oil-based paints are known for their durability and ability to provide a smooth finish. However, they have a strong odor and require mineral spirits for cleanup. Both types cater to different consumer preferences based on factors like ease of use, durability, and environmental impact.</p></p>
