<p><p>In the limo software market, there are two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and maintained locally on the user's computer system. It offers greater control and customization but requires more resources and maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based software is accessed through the internet and hosted on remote servers. It provides flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates, but may have limited customization options. Both types have their pros and cons, so businesses can choose the option that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
