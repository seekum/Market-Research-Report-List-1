<p><p>The underground water leak detection service market can be divided into two main types: invasive leak detection and non-invasive underground leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves physically excavating the area to access the pipes and identify the leak. This method is effective but can be time-consuming and expensive. On the other hand, non-invasive underground leak detection uses advanced technologies like acoustic sensors and thermal imaging to locate leaks without the need for excavation. This method is faster, more cost-effective, and less disruptive to the surrounding area.</p></p>
