<p><p>Car Keyless Go market consists of two main types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). </p><p>PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without having to take their key fob out of their pocket or purse. The system relies on proximity sensors to detect the key fob nearby. </p><p>RKES, on the other hand, requires the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock or start the vehicle remotely. This type of system offers convenience and security benefits to car owners.</p></p>
