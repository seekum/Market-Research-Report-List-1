<p><p>The Saas (Software as a Service) based Human Resource Management (HRM) market can be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise HRM refers to software that is installed and operated on the company's server or infrastructure, requiring maintenance and updates from the organization's IT team. In contrast, cloud-based HRM operates on the vendor's servers and is accessed through the internet, eliminating the need for on-site installation, maintenance, and updates. Cloud-based HRM offers greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, making it a popular choice for organizations of all sizes.</p></p>
