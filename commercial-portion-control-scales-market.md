<p><p>Commercial portion control scales are available in two main types: electric and mechanical. Electric scales are powered by electricity and feature digital displays for accurate readings. They are often preferred for their durability and reliability in commercial food service settings. Both types of scales are essential for accurately measuring and portioning ingredients in food service businesses.</p></p>
