<p><p>Colorants for plastics market can be classified into two main types: inorganic pigments and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments are minerals that are ground into fine particles and provide bright, high-opacity colors to plastics. They are known for their durability and resistance to heat and light. On the other hand, organic pigments are derived from natural sources or synthetic compounds. They offer a wide range of color options and are often preferred for their transparency and brightness. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the aesthetic appeal of plastic products.</p></p>
